Cybercrime in Germany

Cybercrime in Germany

  • 1. German Parliament Breach (2020)

    In May 2020, the German Parliament noticed a massive data breach. The hackers managed to gain access to the parliamentary network and steal confidential data. It was later revealed that the hackers had used a vulnerability in the network to gain access to the system.

  • 2. City of Wolfsburg Data Breach (2019)

    In April 2019, the City of Wolfsburg suffered a data breach, which affected the personal data of over 20,000 individuals. The attackers were able to gain access to the city’s IT system through a vulnerability in the software. The attack was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 3. German Steel Company Data Breach (2019)

    In August 2019, the German steel company ThyssenKrupp suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s research and development projects. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 4. Equifax Data Breach (2019)

    In September 2019, the consumer credit reporting agency Equifax suffered a major data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s systems and steal the personal data of over 145 million individuals. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 5. German Banking Data Breach (2020)

    In January 2020, a group of hackers managed to gain access to the systems of several German banks and steal the personal data of over 800,000 customers. The hackers were able to gain access to the banks’ systems by exploiting a vulnerability in the software. The attack was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 6. German Automaker Data Breach (2020)

    In February 2020, the German automaker Volkswagen suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s research and development projects. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 7. German Stock Exchange Data Breach (2019)

    In April 2019, the German stock exchange Deutsche Börse suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s trading activities. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 8. German Email Provider Data Breach (2019)

    In May 2019, the German email provider GMX suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s customers. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 9. German Retailer Data Breach (2019)

    In July 2019, the German retailer Lidl suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s customers. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.

  • 10. German Energy Company Data Breach (2019)

    In August 2019, the German energy company RWE suffered a data breach. The attackers managed to gain access to the company’s internal network and steal data related to the company’s customers. The breach was discovered only after the hackers had already stolen the data.
