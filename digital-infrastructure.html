Digital Infrastructure in Germany

Germany has been a leader in investing in and developing its digital infrastructure. The government has invested heavily in building out its telecoms infrastructure, rolling out high-speed fibre connections and 5G networks across the country. It has also been investing in digital technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and quantum computing.
